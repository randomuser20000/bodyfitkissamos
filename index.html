Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Bodyfit Kissamos located?

Bodyfit Kissamos is located in Kissamos, a coastal town in the Chania regional unit of western Crete, Greece. The gym is easily accessible from the town center and serves both local residents and tourists visiting the area.

How much does a gym pass cost?

A day pass costs €5 with full floor access. Weekly passes are €25, monthly memberships €40, and annual memberships €390 for the best value. Longer plans also available: 2 months €70, 3 months €100, 6 months €190. Cash, cards, and NFC payments accepted at the desk.

What are the opening hours?

Bodyfit Kissamos is open Monday through Friday from 10:00 to 22:00 and Saturday from 10:00 to 21:00. The gym is closed on Sundays for recovery. These hours run year-round including the summer tourist season.

Is Bodyfit Kissamos good for tourists?

Yes, Bodyfit Kissamos is ideal for travelers visiting Crete who want to maintain their training routine. The weekly pass (€25) is designed specifically for tourists, offering 7 consecutive days of full access.
